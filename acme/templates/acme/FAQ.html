Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

To report a technical fault:

  1. Log in to your account on our support portal
  2. Navigate to "Technical Support" section
  3. Fill out the fault report form with details
  4. Attach any relevant logs or documentation
  5. Our support team will respond within 24 hours

Our standard warranty includes:

  • 3-year limited hardware warranty
  • 1-year software support
  • 90-day replacement guarantee

Contact support@acme.com for warranty claims.

Typical lead times:

  • Prototypes: 3-5 business days
  • Small batches (1-100 units): 7-10 days
  • High-volume orders: 2-3 weeks

Expedited service available for 25% rush fee.

All PCBs undergo:

  • Automated Optical Inspection (AOI)
  • Electrical continuity testing
  • Impedance control (for high-frequency boards)
  • X-ray layer alignment check
  • Final visual inspection

We accept:

  • Gerber (RS-274X preferred)
  • IPC-2581
  • ODB++
  • Eagle/Altium/KiCad native files

  • RoHS Directive 2015/863
  • REACH SVHC compliance
  • IPC-A-600 Class 2/3 standards
  • UL 94V-0 flammability rating

We ship globally via:

  • DHL/FedEx Express (1-3 days)
  • Sea freight (for bulk orders)
  • Customs documentation included

Export licenses provided for restricted materials.
