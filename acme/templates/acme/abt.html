About Our Company

ACME Manufacturing Corp. is a trusted leader in the design and manufacturing of advanced printed circuit boards (PCBs) and microcontroller solutions. With a commitment to precision engineering and innovation, we deliver high-performance 32-bit and 64-bit microcontroller boards, along with a wide range of custom and industry-standard PCB assemblies. Our products power a broad spectrum of applications across industrial automation, IoT systems, embedded devices, and consumer electronics. At ACME, we pride ourselves on providing scalable, reliable, and efficient hardware solutions tailored to meet the evolving needs of modern technology ecosystems. Driven by a "Brighter Through Innovation" philosophy, we combine state-of-the-art manufacturing processes with a dedicated team of engineers and developers. This synergy allows us to offer cutting-edge board designs and manufacturing services that support the future of connected devices and intelligent systems. From prototyping to full-scale production, ACME Manufacturing Corp. is your partner in building the next generation of electronic innovation.
